AIESEC - GLOBAL PASSPORT

Introduction

PROJECT CONCEPT: Global Passport is a national project run by AIESEC Vietnam with the aim to enhance English communication skills, cutural understanding and self-confidence for students and recent graduates.

BACKGROUND: The Vietnamese government has identified English speaking as the key to the country's future growth ambitions because of the strong influence of globalization on its economics, especially employment.

ACTIVITIES: GP organizes 6-week course specialized for University Undergraduates & workers, with the main content of the class is Skills and Culture. Interns from all around the world with good English command will help students with their soft skills (presenting, team work, communicating...) and introduce their culture at the same time. Besides the course works, there will also be sides event such as Field trips, Camping Day, Amazing Race.

Impact

Start from 2012
Operate in 3 biggest cities of Vietnam: Ho Chi Minh, Ha Noi, Da Nang
Deliver more than 80 classes nationally, impacting 700 - 800 students a year
Every year we bring the bridge connecting more than 100 international interns from 30 countries to Vietnam culture.
