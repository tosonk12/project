SOUTHEST ASIA BEYOND 2015

Bootstrap Slider

"Southeast Asia Beyond 2015 And The World We Want" is a youth engagement and development programme created in support of the World We Want 2015 campaign for Southeast Asia and the ASEAN region. Its prime objectives are to "equip, empower & enable" urban, rural and indigenous youths to realise the transformative results of the Sustainable Development Goals and the shared objectives of ASEAN for a more inclusive sustainable and sociality integrated ASEAN.

The program is initiated by Brand4Humanity & co-organized with partners whose projects are supporting youth to develop social awareness & promote mutual understanding in ASEAN
