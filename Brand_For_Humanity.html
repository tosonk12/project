Brand For Humanity

Introduction

Brand4Humanity is a social enterprise specialising in cause branding and cause marketing campaigns. Campaigns are designed to address real social and environmental issues.

We work alongside local & international organizations serving in the areas of

  1. YOUTHS ENGAGEMENT & DEVELOPMENT;
  2. LITERACY, HUMAN DEVELOPMENT & WELLFARE (Elderly, autism, vulnerable women & children);
  3. DISASTER RELIEF
  4. SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT & ENVIRONMENT
  5. WOMEN'S EMPOWERMENT
  6. HEALTH & NUTRITION
  7. INCLUSIVE GROWTH/ DEVELOPMENT THROUGH SOCIAL ENTERPRISES & IMPACT INVESTMENTS
